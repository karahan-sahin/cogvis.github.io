Understanding the World
Through Vision & Cognition

The CogVis Lab develops computational methods that enable machines to perceive, reason about, and interact with the 3D visual world — drawing inspiration from human cognition to build the next generation of embodied visual intelligence.

Our Research Meet the Team
What we do

Research Areas

We work at the intersection of computer vision, machine learning, and cognitive science to build systems that truly understand the visual world.

01
3D Scene Understanding

Recovering rich 3D geometric and semantic representations of scenes from images and video, including dynamic scene flow and object permanence.

02
Embodied AI

Building agents that navigate, reason, and interact in real and simulated 3D environments using language, vision, and affordance grounding.

03
Video Understanding

Long-form temporal reasoning, action recognition, and causal event modeling in unconstrained real-world video streams.

04
Medical Vision

Self-supervised depth estimation and semantic segmentation for surgical and endoscopic imaging, enabling safer clinical procedures.
